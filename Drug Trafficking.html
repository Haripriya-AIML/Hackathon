Software Solution to Detect Drug Trafficking on Telegram, WhatsApp & Instagram

About

This cyber security tool aims to identify users behind Telegram, WhatsApp, and Instagram channels used for drug trafficking in India. It detects suspicious activity and attempts to triangulate identifying information such as IP address, phone number, and email ID.
